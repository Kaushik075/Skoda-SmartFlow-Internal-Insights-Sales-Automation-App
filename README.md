# Skoda-SmartFlow-Internal-Insights-Sales-Automation-App
## Project Type
**Consulting Prototype & Technical Documentation**  
_Built as an interview assessment for Mahavir Group (Skoda Division)_

---

## Executive Summary

**Skoda SmartFlow is an internal insights application and prototype end-to-end data warehouse and automation system,** designed for Mahavir Skoda’s CEO, IT Head, and leadership team. With one-click refresh, the app delivers instant, real-time business insights, enabling management to make decisions with full operational transparency.

Built as a consulting demonstration, SmartFlow empowers sales and marketing teams to capture, process, and manage all dealership leads digitally—with minimal manual effort and a secure backend database. The system streamlines lead qualification, interactions, and reporting, helping reduce turnaround time and operational errors.

---

## Business Use Case & Problem Statement

Legacy Excel/manual workflows led to:
- 24+ hour delays for sales and performance reports
- High data entry errors and risk-prone manual tasks
- No unified, real-time view for leadership or teams

**SmartFlow Solution:**
- **Leadership Dashboard:** CEOs/IT Heads access instant KPIs and funnel insights with a single refresh
- **Sales/Marketing Workflow Automation:** Teams track and manage all leads digitally; eliminated manual data entry
- **Security:** Single, centralized, and protected MySQL database
- **Scalability:** Blueprint for extension across multiple brands and branches

---

## Core Deliverables

- **System Architecture:** 3-layer MySQL data warehouse (Bronze/Silver/Gold) with modular ETL
- **Full Automation:** n8n & OpenAI-driven lead management, calendar sync, and workflow alerts
- **Dashboards:** Power BI templates for live KPIs
- **Consulting Documentation:** Implementation playbook, process mapping, technical designs

---

## Key Impacts

- 96% reduction in reporting delay (24 hours → <1 hour)
- 16–24 manual hours saved daily across teams
- 90% reduction in data entry errors
- Instant visibility for leadership—insights on demand
